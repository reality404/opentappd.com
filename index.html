Summary of Untappd

Untappd is a popular and highly regarded platform for beer enthusiasts, offering a comprehensive experience for discovering, rating, and sharing beer. However, Untappd now requires a commercial agreement for API access. As part of this policy change, existing API tokens may no longer work.

Additionally, while users can export their data from Untappd, this feature is restricted unless you are an Untappd Insider or reside in the European Union or the European Economic Area (EEA). Below, you will find alternative sites for beer rating and instructions on how to export data from Untappd if you are an Insider or based in the EU/EEA.

How to Get Data from Untappd

To access your data from Untappd, follow the appropriate link below based on your location:

  • If you are an Untappd insider, visit the following page to learn how to export your data: Exportable Data Feature
  • If you are in the EU, visit the following page to understand how to manage your privacy and access your data: Privacy on Untappd

Alternative Websites

  • RateBeer As of February 2019, RateBeer is fully owned by ZX Ventures, AB InBev's global growth and innovation arm.
  • BeerAdvocate In February 2020, the site was acquired by Next Glass, the parent company of Untappd.
  • Pint Please Pint Please is a Finnish beer rating and social platform.
